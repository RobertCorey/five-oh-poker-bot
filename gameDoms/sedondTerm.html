Introducing the Outs Counter
We’ve all been there: you’ve got a few seconds left to place a bet and you’re scanning a table full of cards to figure out how many outs you have... STRESS! So, to make your lives easier, we’ve built a new feature that will save you time and improve your game performance.
This new premium feature will show you what cards are already out on the table and which ones are still hiding in the deck. Although anyone could figure this out by just looking at the table, the Outs Counter helps you visualize this better, save crucial time, and improve your performance. The green lights indicate cards that are not on the table yet, and if the green light is off, the card is already on the table.
This means you can play a more educated game – instead of taking risky bets, you can easily count how many outs you have, and therefore how likely you are to win the hand or game.
To launch this new premium feature, we are giving every single player a two day trial. After that, you can purchase as many days of using the Outs Counter as you want.
Try it out, you’ll see the difference straight away!
